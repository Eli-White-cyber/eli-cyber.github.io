FCC Withdraws Telecom Cybersecurity Mandates After Salt Typhoon Hack

November 20, 2025

In response to the Salt Typhoon cyberattack, the FCC has voted to withdraw previously approved mandatory cybersecurity requirements for U.S. telecommunications providers. Instead, telecom companies have committed to voluntary improvements to their cyber defenses.
