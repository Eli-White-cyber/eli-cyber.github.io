About Me

Hello! I’m Eli White, a senior Cybersecurity major and Computer Science minor at Anderson University with a 3.5 GPA. I am passionate about understanding cyber threats, analyzing vulnerabilities, and applying cybersecurity best practices to real-world problems.

I hold CompTIA Security+ and Network+ certifications, and I am actively working towards my Cisco CCNA and CompTIA CySA+ certifications. My hands-on experience includes a city government IT internship, where I have gained practical skills in network security, threat analysis, and IT operations.

My technical skill set includes: Cybersecurity Analysis, Networking, Network Security, Incident Response, Cisco Configurations, Python, Risk Assessment. I document my work and share insights on Medium and maintain a professional presence on LinkedIn.

My career goal is to leverage my skills in cyber intelligence and digital investigations, with the aim of contributing as a cyber-specialist at agencies like HSI or the FBI. This blog serves as a portfolio of my research, analysis, and ongoing learning in the cybersecurity field.
